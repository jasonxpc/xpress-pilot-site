What Affects Your Price

Why every quote is unique

Print pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. These are the key factors we consider when building your exact quote.

📦
Quantity

More units = lower cost per piece. We'll often show you pricing at multiple quantities so you can find the sweet spot for your budget.

📄
Paper Stock

From economy 60lb text to premium 16PT cover stock — the paper weight and type significantly impact the final price and feel of the product.

✨
Coating & Finish

Gloss UV, matte laminate, and soft-touch each have different costs. We'll recommend the best finish for your project and budget.

📐
Size & Complexity

Standard sizes cost less than custom. Die-cutting, folding, and binding add to the total — but we'll always give you the full breakdown upfront.

⚡
Turnaround Speed

Standard 3–5 day jobs are most economical. Rush and same-day service are available with a small premium. We'll always be upfront about rush fees.

🎨
Color vs. B&W

Full 4-color printing (CMYK) costs more than black-and-white, but we offer competitive rates for both. Ask us about hybrid options too.
