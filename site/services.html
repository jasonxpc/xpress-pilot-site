File Preparation

How to prepare your files

Follow these guidelines for best results. Not sure? Upload what you have and we'll let you know.

1
Set up at correct size

Set your document to the final trim size. Include 0.125" bleed on all sides if your design extends to the edge. Keep text and important elements 0.125" inside the trim.

Ask us if you need a template →

2
Export as PDF (preferred)

Save as a high-resolution PDF at 300 DPI minimum. Flatten transparency and embed all fonts. PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4 formats work best for offset and digital print.

We also accept AI, PSD, INDD, TIFF, JPG

3
Upload or email to us

Use our secure FilesAnywhere upload portal or email your files to info@xpressprinting.com. We'll review your file and confirm it's press-ready before printing.
