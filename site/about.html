How It Started

Built from the ground up in Irvine

Jason [Last Name] founded Xpress Printing in 1989 with a single press and a simple belief: local businesses deserve professional-quality printing without the big-city wait times or pricing. Starting from a small shop in Irvine, the business grew through word of mouth — one satisfied client at a time.

Today

35 years later, still local. Still hands-on.

We've grown our capabilities and our team, but our commitment hasn't changed. Every job that comes through our door is reviewed by an experienced printer — not a bot, not an algorithm. We catch file problems before they become print problems. We answer the phone. We know your name.
